Welcome to the Community Building Timebank. This is the place to be if you're interested in developing your community. Here you'll learn more about how the Timebanking concept works.

Timebanking is a fantastic way to exchange skills and resources without any money involved. This concept is based solely on time. With Timebanking, you help others with what you love to do, and you'll have the opportunity to ask for help when you need it yourself!

For every hour you spend helping someone, you earn the same amount of hours back. The hours you "earn" are stored in your Timebank. You can then use those hours to ask others enrolled in this project for help or assistance with something you need.

To give you an example: You could shovel snow for the eldery couple in the neighborhood, spending 2 hours of your time. In return, that same couple (or someone else that is a part of the community) will use 2 hours of their time to babysit your kids, while you and your wife go out for a long-awaited dinner, earning them 2 credits back.

It's a win-win situation. If this sounds interesting to YOU, then hop on board and make your community a better place! :)
